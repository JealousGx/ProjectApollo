Board template for Apollo controller boards

PCB design template:
- 2-layer
- PC104 SAMTEC connector (4x26) 
- 88 mm x 88 mm = 7744 sq.mm (compatible with Eagle Freeware restrictions)

Credit: 
- Base board template derived from the PCB design of HuskySat-1 satellite developed by University of Washington in collaboration with Raisbeck Aviation High School, Quick2space, AMSAT and others
